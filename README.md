### Additional material for [*Inland Multi-Ship Trajectory Prediction: A Comparative Study of Graph Neural Network-Based Models*](https://kadnt.github.io/GNN-IV/link_to_paper.html) (Donandt, K. et al., 2025). 
The visualization were created using the coastline, fairway, and waterway profile layers of IENC charts, downloaded from [IENC WSV](https://www.elwis.de/DE/dynamisch/IENC/). Kilometer marks and curvature values at the fairway center, as defined in the [paper](https://kadnt.github.io/GNN-IV/link_to_paper.html), were added based on fairway boundary data provided by the Federal Waterways Engineering and Research Institute ([BAW](https://www.google.com/url?sa=t&source=web&rct=j&opi=89978449&url=https://www.baw.de/en/die_baw/die_baw.html&ved=2ahUKEwjghtCR9dyQAxWAhP0HHYzGHzgQFnoECCAQAQ&usg=AOvVaw3mWZifZ5UH7csVchMRj6tA)).
